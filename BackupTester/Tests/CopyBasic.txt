.          2014-01-01          2014-01-01 -A---- source [1]
.          2014-01-02          2014-01-02 -A---- target [2]

.

.The file '%WORKSPACE%\target' already exists. (%WORKSPACE%\source)

.          2014-01-02          2014-01-02 -A---- target [2]
endlist
